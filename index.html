What are the eight values?

There are four independent axes - Economic, Government, Politics, and Diplomatic - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

Socialist

Those with higher Equality scores believe the economy should distribute value evenly among the populace. They tend to support progressive tax codes, social programs, and at high values, socialism.

ECONOMIC

Markets

Those with higher Market scores believe the economy should be focused on rapid growth. They tend to support lower taxes, privatization, deregulation, and at high values, laissez-faire capitalism.

Conservative

Those with higher Conservative scores belive that the government should return to older systems, they belive that wintercrest should listen to older guidence for what the direction should be.

Government

Reform

Those with higher reform scores belive that wintercrest should change how the government works, sometimes on a fundamntal level, without looking to older ideas.

Restricted

Those with higher Restricted scores belive that parties need to be restricted, and that there are too many parties. At the extreeme, they belive in a single governing party.

Politics

Freedom

Those with higher Party Freedom scores belive that parties should be able to operate with no or little restriction.

Active

Those with higher Active scores belive that wintercrest should be much more active on the NS stage.

Forign Affairs

Isolation

Those with higher Isolation Scores belive that Wintercrest should focus on what happens internally, and be less active in forign affairs


What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results?

In addition to matching you to the eight values, the quiz also attempts to match you to a political party.
